About Dr. Farhat Hashmi

Dr. Farhat Hashmi is a globally respected Islamic scholar, author, and founder of Al-Huda International Welfare Foundation. With a PhD in Islamic Studies from the University of Glasgow, she has dedicated her life to making Quranic education accessible for everyone.

Her unique teaching methodology emphasizes understanding the Quran directly in Arabic, combined with Tafsir and practical application. Millions have benefited from her lectures, courses, and online programs across the world.

Our Online Quran Academy is an extension of her vision — offering structured, one-on-one online classes with certified Ijazah teachers. We serve students of all ages, from beginners to advanced learners, with flexible schedules and a nurturing Islamic environment.

  • 20+ years of global teaching experience
  • Over 50,000 students graduated worldwide
  • Separate male & female instructors available
  • 24/7 student support & progress tracking
